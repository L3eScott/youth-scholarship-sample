Donate to a Student

Every dollar goes to tuition, books, and fees — not to overhead. The church covers the administrative side.

What your gift does

A scholarship from this ministry typically covers between $500 and $2,000 of a student's annual expenses. Some students get a single one-time gift to bridge a gap. Others are sponsored across multiple semesters.

You can give once, monthly, or annually. Whatever fits.

Tax-Deductible — and We'll Send the Receipt

The SJ Youth Ministry operates under the 501(c)(3) status of St. James Baptist Church. Your gift is fully tax-deductible. You'll receive:

  • An emailed receipt for every online gift
  • An annual giving statement in January for the previous year
  • A handwritten thank-you note for sponsorship-level gifts

Where the Money Goes

  • 100% of your gift goes to scholarship awards. The church covers all administrative costs.
  • Awards go directly to schools — paid to the institution, not to the student — so the money is always used for what it was given for.
  • We track every dollar. Annual giving report is available on request.

Want to do more than give?

We always need mentors. If you'd like to walk with a scholarship student through their season of school, reach out. It's not a heavy commitment — it's a real one.
