A Word From Our Pastor

"Every student deserves someone in their corner."

Senior Pastor

Pastor J. L. Franklin

The SJ Youth Ministry exists because our church believes that education changes futures — and that no young person from our community should have to walk that road alone. We pray for our students by name, we sit with them when they're stuck, and we celebrate every step they take.

If you're a student wondering whether to apply, please apply. If you're someone wondering whether your gift matters, it does — every dollar goes to a student.

How We Help

Three things every student in our program gets.

💵

Financial Support

Direct scholarship funds paid to the student's school or program. Tuition, books, supplies, fees — whatever the school invoices.

🤝

A Mentor

Every student we sponsor is paired with someone from our church family. A real human being who knows their name, prays for them, and is in their corner.

🙏

A Praying Church

Our congregation prays for our scholarship students by name — every Sunday morning and every Wednesday night. You're not on this journey alone.

Who We Serve

Students from our community pursuing real education.

We support students at every stage:

  • Graduating high school seniors heading to a 4-year college, community college, or trade school.
  • Current college students who need help bridging a gap or covering an unexpected cost.
  • Adults returning to school — finishing a degree, starting a new career, getting certified.

You don't have to be a member of St. James to apply. You just need to be from our community and serious about the next step in your education.
