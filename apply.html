Who Can Apply

  • Graduating high school seniors heading to college, community college, trade school, or certification program.
  • Current college or trade school students who need help covering tuition, books, or fees.
  • Adults returning to school — finishing a degree, starting a new career, or getting certified.

You don't need to be a member of St. James Baptist Church. You need to have some connection to our community.

What You'll Need

Before you start the application, gather these:

  1. A short essay (one page is plenty). Tell us about you: what you're planning to study, why, and what you'd do with this opportunity.
  2. One letter of recommendation from someone who knows you and isn't a family member. A teacher, a coach, a pastor, a boss, a coworker.
  3. Proof of enrollment or acceptance letter from the school you're attending (or planning to).
  4. Cost summary from the school — tuition, books, fees. Whatever they invoice.
  5. Your contact info — name, phone, email, and one parent or guardian's contact if you're under 18.

If any of these feel hard to put together — call us. We'd rather help you put together a complete application than have you give up because of a logistics problem.

How to Apply

  1. Fill out the application form below (or download the PDF).
  2. Send your essay, recommendation, proof of enrollment, and cost summary by email to scholarship@stjamesbaptistchurch.org — or drop them off at the church office.
